### 001. 大理寺少卿游 | Strange Tales of Tang Dynasty: Assistant Minister of Justice
  - 古装 · 探案 · 轻悬疑 · 群像
  - Costume · Detective · Light Mystery · Ensemble Cast

---
* **主演介绍 | Main Cast**
  - **丁禹兮（Ding Yuxi）｜饰 李饼（Li Bing）**
    - 大理寺少卿，外冷内热，逻辑缜密
    - Assistant Minister of Justice, calm, sharp-minded, and highly analytical

  - **周奇（Zhou Qi）｜饰 陈拾（Chen Shi）**
    - 少年随从，心思单纯但观察力惊人
    - Young aide, innocent yet surprisingly perceptive

  - **魏哲鸣（Wei Zheming）｜饰 王七（Wang Qi）**
    - 行事洒脱，擅长江湖人脉
    - Easygoing, well-connected in the martial world

  - **张奕聪（Zhang Yicong）｜饰 阿里巴巴（Ali Baba）**
    - 异域背景，思维跳脱
    - Exotic background, unconventional thinking

---
* **剧情介绍 | Plot Summary**
  - 该剧改编自漫画《大理寺日志》，以唐代为背景
  - Adapted from the manhua *Records of the Great Court of Justice*, set in the Tang Dynasty

  - 围绕大理寺少卿李饼展开一系列离奇案件
  - Follows Li Bing as he investigates a series of bizarre and mysterious cases

  - 每个案件看似荒诞，实则暗藏人性与权力博弈
  - Each case appears absurd on the surface but hides deep human motives and power struggles

  - 破案过程融合推理、幽默与群像成长
  - The investigation blends deduction, humor, and character development

---
* **作品特色 | Highlights**
  - 轻悬疑 + 漫改风格，节奏明快
  - Light mystery with a strong manhua-inspired aesthetic

  - 群像塑造鲜明，每个角色各司其职
  - Well-defined ensemble cast, each with a clear role

  - 探案之外，更关注友情与信任
  - Beyond crime-solving, it emphasizes friendship and trust
