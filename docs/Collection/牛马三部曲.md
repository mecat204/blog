### 自由的探索
* **自由的探索这个世界，大概就是我所追求的理想生活吧。草原，丛林，山间，雪原，丘陵，沙漠。就像自由的探索提瓦特大陆一样**
* **The ideal life I long for is the freedom to explore this world — the grasslands, the jungles, the mountains, the snowfields, the hills, the desert — just like freely wandering across the land of Teyvat.**
