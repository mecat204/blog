  - <font color="#F8791B"> A lew concerning food contamination [con- <font color="#F8791B">tami- <font color="#F8791B">nation] must</font> be made. 必须制定一项关于食品污染的法律</font>。

* **regarding** prep. **<font color="#0F3194">【有关, 关于】</font>**
* **<font color="#0F3194">【尤用于信函或演说中】</font>**
  - <font color="#F8791B"> Regarding your recent enquiry, I have enclosed a copy of our user guide. 关于您最近的咨询, 我随信附上一份我</font>们的用户指南。
  - <font color="#F8791B"> I'll make no comments regarding this question. 对于这一问题我不作评论。</font>

* **respecting** prep. **<font color="#0F3194">【关于, 鉴于】</font>**
* **<font color="#0F3194">【与concerning 基本同义, 常可换用。】</font>**
  - <font color="#F8791B"> I have nothing to say respecting/concerning the matter. 关于这件事, 我无话可说。</font>
  - <font color="#F8791B"> Respecting/concerning the fact that the accused was a mintor, the charges were dropped. 鉴于被告是未成年人,</font> 起诉被撤销。

  - <font color="#F8791B"> Certainly, he is a loser. 当然, 他是个输家。</font>

* **surely** adv. **<font color="#0F3194">【务必, 无疑】</font>**
